How to create your own Todo List
1. To add a Task to Todo List

First write the name of Task that is needed to be saved into my Task. To do this you need to enter the Task name in the box mentioning "What needs to be done?" After entering the Task name, Click the add button. The Task will be automatically added to list of My Tasks

2. To save the Task to list of My Tasks

Just click on the save button in the left side of screen.

3. To mark the Task as completed

Just click on the checkbox at left side of screen. Automatically the Task will strike out and it can be considered that the Task is completed.

4. To delete a Task already present in the list of My Tasks

Just click on delete icon at right side of screen . The Task will be deleted from the List of my tasks.

But always take care to save the Task before reloading the page!!
